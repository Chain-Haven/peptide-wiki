# PeptideWiki — SEO Content Roadmap

This is the content plan that turns the technical SEO foundation into rankings and
traffic. Priorities are grounded in live SERP analysis (June 2026): for the
highest-value peptide queries, the first page is dominated by **modest-authority
medspa and vendor blogs** — which a focused, well-cited, tool-backed reference
site can outrank.

## How we win (the thesis)

1. **We already have the tools competitors rank with** — a reconstitution/dosage
   calculator and a side-by-side comparison. Wrapping those in keyword-targeted
   guide content is the fastest path to ranking.
2. **Topical authority** — a dense web of guides that all interlink with our 60+
   peptide pages signals expertise to Google for the whole "peptides" topic.
3. **Commercial intent → affiliate revenue** — "best peptides for X" listicles
   capture buyers and funnel them to our peptide pages and vendor links.

## Content clusters (priority order)

### 1. How-to / process guides — HIGH priority, very winnable
Searchers want steps; SERPs are vendor blogs. We have the calculators.
- ✅ How to Reconstitute Peptides (Step-by-Step) — `/guides/how-to-reconstitute-peptides`
- How to Inject Peptides Subcutaneously (insulin syringe guide)
- How to Store Peptides (lyophilized vs reconstituted, travel)
- Reading a Certificate of Analysis (COA) for Peptides
- Bacteriostatic Water vs Sterile Water: What's the Difference

### 2. Per-peptide dosage guides — HIGH priority, recurring template
"[peptide] dosage" has demand for nearly every compound; SERPs are clinic blogs.
- ✅ BPC-157 Dosage Guide — `/guides/bpc-157-dosage-guide`
- TB-500 Dosage Guide
- Ipamorelin / CJC-1295 Dosage Guide
- Semaglutide Dosage & Titration Schedule
- Tirzepatide Dosage & Titration Schedule
- Tesamorelin, MK-677, Retatrutide, AOD-9604 dosage guides

### 3. "Best peptides for [goal]" listicles — HIGH priority, monetizes
Commercial-investigation intent → affiliate. SERPs include Innerbody, GoodRx.
- ✅ Best Peptides for Weight Loss — `/guides/best-peptides-for-weight-loss`
- ✅ Best Peptides for Healing & Injury Recovery — `/guides/best-peptides-for-healing-recovery`
- Best Peptides for Muscle Growth
- Best Peptides for Anti-Aging & Longevity
- Best Peptides for Skin / Hair
- Best GLP-1 Peptides Compared

### 4. Comparison pages — MEDIUM priority, strong long-tail
"[A] vs [B]" — we have the comparison tool + trial data.
- ✅ Semaglutide vs Tirzepatide — `/guides/semaglutide-vs-tirzepatide`
- BPC-157 vs TB-500
- Ipamorelin vs Sermorelin vs CJC-1295
- Retatrutide vs Tirzepatide
- MK-677 vs Ipamorelin

### 5. Stack guides — MEDIUM priority
"[stack] protocol" has dedicated demand (e.g. "wolverine stack").
- ✅ BPC-157 + TB-500 "Wolverine" Stack — `/guides/bpc-157-tb-500-wolverine-stack`
- GH stack: CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin
- Recomp / body composition stack
- Anti-aging / longevity stack

### 6. Safety / explainer content — supports E-E-A-T
- Are Peptides Legal? (US + international overview)
- Peptide Side Effects: What the Research Shows
- What Are Research Peptides? (beginner primer)
- Peptides and WADA / Drug Testing

## Cadence & measurement
- Target 2–4 new guides/week; refresh top performers quarterly.
- Every guide interlinks to ≥3 peptide pages + a tool (calculator/compare) + 1–2 sibling guides.
- After publishing, track in Google Search Console (impressions → position → CTR);
  double down on pages reaching positions 5–15 ("striking distance").

## Off-page (not code — owner action)
- Digital PR / outreach for backlinks (the biggest external ranking lever).
- Reddit (r/Peptides), forums, and community presence linking back to guides/tools.
- A named author/medical reviewer byline to strengthen YMYL trust signals.
